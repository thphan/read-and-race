How to play?

◁ Back to Home
  1. Press ⏎ key to get a random letter.
  2. Let the little buddy pronounce the letter.
  3. If the answer is correct, press ▶ key to move the car forward.
    Otherwise, press ◀ key to move the car backward.
  4. You win when you reach the Finish line.

You can reset the game anytime by pressing "r" key.
